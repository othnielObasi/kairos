What Execution History means
This page is the governed action and outcome log. It shows what Kairos executed, when it opened or closed an action, the resulting PnL, and the supporting artifact or settlement reference. It is not the same as the payment ledger.
What this page answers
What action did Kairos take, when did it close, and what was the outcome?
What Transactions answers
What got paid or settled on Arc, and which receipt proves it?
How they relate
One execution can appear here, generate Kairos invoice, receipt, and delivery-proof pages, and also produce a separate payment receipt in Transactions.
