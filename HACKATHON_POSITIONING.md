# Hackathon Positioning

Actura is aimed most strongly at these prize lanes:

- **Best Validation & Trust Model**
- **Best Compliance & Risk Guardrails**
- **Best Trustless Trading Agent**

This is because the project emphasizes:

- deterministic validation artifacts
- capital governance
- explainability
- supervisory control
- ERC-8004 trust alignment

## Current Readiness

Actura already implements the **core runtime**, **trust model**, **dashboard**, **governance logic**, and **ERC-8004 adapter layer**.

The remaining steps to go fully live inside the hackathon environment are to plug in the official registry, router, vault, and validator details once they are released.

## Demo Flow

Recommended demo sequence:

1. show market signal and regime state  
2. select a trade from the decision table  
3. open the Trade Trust Proof  
4. show the Capital Rights Visualizer  
5. show execution simulation + oracle integrity  
6. show operator controls  
7. show ERC-8004 protocol panel  
8. explain how the agent becomes fully live once official infra addresses are plugged in
